How to connect the map to WiFi:

  • Connect the map to power (the light should go white)
  • Connect to the network called "Map_Config" with your phone
  • Sign in or go to 192.168.4.1
  • Select the network and enter the password
  • If it connects, the map will flash green

Map Key:

    Clear Sky - Light Off

    Few Clouds - 10% White

    Scattered Clouds - 30% White

    Broken Clouds - 50% White

    Shower Rain - Light Blue

    Rain - Blue

    Thunderstorm - White Flashing

    Snow - White

    Mist - Green/Blue
