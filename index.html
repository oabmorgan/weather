How to connect the map to WiFi:

  • Connect to the network called "Map_Config" with your phone
  • Sign in or go to 192.168.4.1
  • Select the network and enter the password

Map Key:

  • Off - Clear Sky

  • White (Dim) - Light Clouds
  • White (Low) - Cloudy
  • White (Medium) - Heavy Clouds
  • White (High) - Overcast

  • White (Pulse) - Snow
  • Blue (Slow Pulse) - Drizzle
  • Blue (Med Pulse) - Light Rain
  • Blue (Med Pulse) - Rain
  • Blue (Fast Pulse) - Heavy Rain
  • White (Flashing) - Thunderstorm
  • Purple - Storm

  • Blue (Dim) - Mist
